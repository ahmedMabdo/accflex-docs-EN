The program compares the data of the [Estimated Budget](../settings/estimated-budget.md) with the actual budgets. Different types of estimated budget reports can be extracted from the program:

* [Estimated Budget by Period](estimated-budget-reports.md#estimated-budget-by-period).
* [Estimated Budget by Month](estimated-budget-reports.md#estimated-budget-by-month).
* [Estimated Budget by Day](estimated-budget-reports.md#estimated-budget-by-day).
