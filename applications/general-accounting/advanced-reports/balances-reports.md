### Trail Balance Reports

{% tabs %}
{% tab title="Installed Program" %}

The program enables extracting trial balance reports in different forms, depending on the data collection method:

* Data collection by account level.
* Data collection by last level accounts.

{% hint style="info" %}
Level of accounts, is the number of figures of the accounts number, for example: account no. 11 is a level 2, account no. 111 is a level 3.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

For data collection by account level, there are two types of reports, with different displayed data:&#x20;

| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Adv 06.png>) | Report displays balances of accounts for starting and ending periods.                      |
| ------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Adv 07.png>) | Report displays balances of accounts for starting and ending periods, with summary report. |

To view and print trail balances report, set the level of accounts, enter the period of time data, and branches, then click **Preview**/ **Print**. Filter the search results: enter journal type and status, also, you can hide customers, or suppliers accounts.

To view and print trail balances currency equivalent report, enter the period of time data, account level, branches, currency and currency factor, then click **Preview**/ **Print**. Filter the search results: enter journal type and status, also, you can hide customers, or suppliers accounts.

The program will convert all balances to the selected currency, using the entered currency factor.
