Two types of cost centers transactions reports are available:

| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Rep 024.png>) | Report displays name and no. of the cost center, transactions, and sum of balances in local currency.      |
| ------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Rep 025.png>) | Report displays name and no. of the cost center, transactions, and sum of balances of a specific currency. |

To view and print a cost center transactions report, enter the cost center data, period of time, branches, then click **Preview**/ **Print**. Filter the search results: enter data of sub cost centers, journal type and status, also, you can hide customers, or suppliers.
