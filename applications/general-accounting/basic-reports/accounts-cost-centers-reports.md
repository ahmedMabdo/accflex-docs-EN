Different types of account-cost centers transactions reports are available:

| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Rep 041.png>) | Report displays the details of transactions related to the specified account, and the cost center. Also, the sum of balances of the starting, and ending periods, in local currency.         |
| ------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

To view and print account-cost centers transactions report, enter the account data, cost centers, period of time, and branches, then click **Preview**/ **Print**. Filter the search results: enter data of sub accounts, and cost centers, journal type and status, also, you can hide customers, or suppliers.
