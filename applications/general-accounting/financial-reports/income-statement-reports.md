* Comparative Cost Centers Income Statement&#x20;
* Comparative Branches Income Statement

#### Comparative Cost Centers Income Statement

Different reports are available, in different details:

| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Fin 017.png>)     | Report displays data of balances, revenue, and the profit of cost centers for a period of time.             |
| ----------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Fin 018 (2).png>) | Report displays data of balances, estimated budgets, deviation, and previous year balances of cost centers. |
| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Fin 019 (1).png>) | Report displays data of estimated, and actual budgets of cost centers, for a period of time.                |

To view and print Comparative Cost Center Income Statement report. enter the data of period of time, and cost centers, then click **Show**/ **Print**. To filter the search data, set the type and status of journals.
