Different types of Taxes are imposed according to the state tax authority, the General Ledger program helps you register, pay different Taxes and print notices:

* Value Added Tax Notice
* Customer Withholding Tax
* Supplier Withholding Tax
* Supplier Withholding Tax Payment

#### Add a New Notice ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/New.png)

* Enter the **Tax Information**: Notice date, customer/ supplier account, tax account, and journal number.
* Enter the data of the **Tax amount**: currency, amount, and the applied tax according to the Egyptian Tax Authority, or the General Authority for Zakat and Tax. The program will automatically calculate the tax amount, and will use the registered currency factor to display the tax amount in local currency. To edit the currency factor, go to [Currencies](../settings/currencies.md#add-a-new-currency-factor).
* Set the **Invoice/ Bill Information**, and type (Customer, or Supplier Bill).
* Click **Save** ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/Save.png).

{% hint style="info" %}
Tax amount will be zero, if tax exemption was check marked.
{% endhint %}

#### Add a New Receipt ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/New.png)

Enter the data of the withholding tax receipt: type of receipt (Deduct, or Refund), date, branch, customer/ supplier account, tax account, total amount, type of <mark style="color:red;">tax</mark>, and currency, then click Save ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/Save.png). Automatically, the program will display the withholding tax amount, according to the registered tax percentage. To change the withholding tax percentage, go to [System Configuration](../settings/system-configuration.md#source-tax-type).

The program enables registering the payments of the withholding tax for suppliers, to the account of the Egyptian Tax Authority. Enter the start and end of the period, and select a supplier account, then click **Load**.

The program will display the search results, and the details of the receipts: date, total amount, percentage and amount of deduction, and other data. Select the receipts to register the payments, then click **Save**.
