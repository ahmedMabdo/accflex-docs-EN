#### &#x20;New Journal Entry ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/New.png)

To add new journal entries, enter journal's data: Branch, Journal Date and Type, Currency and Journal Description, then enter journal entry details: Accounts' details, Debit or Credit, Branches of entries, then click **Save**. Numbers of journal, archive, serial, and currency factor will be automatically generated, also the journal registering data will be displayed.

#### Template Journal&#x20;

You can add different types of template journals, for frequently used journals, e.g., payroll journals. Enter the journal data then checkmark **Template**, you can recall the template any time.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/JR 04.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Template Checkmark</p></figcaption></figure>

#### Add a New Account ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/new2.png)

The program enables adding new accounts, during the journal entry process. Saving your time, so no need to return back to the chart of accounts for adding a new account. Enter the data of the new account, then **Save**.&#x20;

#### Confirm Journals Entry

This feature is considered the first step of journals revising, for example, a journal was created by an accountant, then confirmed by the accounting manger, and finally the journal was revised by the chief financial officer. To activate this role for a user, go to [User Permissions](../settings/users.md#user-permissions).

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/JR 03 (2).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Confirm a Journal</p></figcaption></figure>

#### Revise Journal ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/JR 03 (1).png>)

You can revise a journal entry directly after creating it, provided that you are permitted to revise journals. To activate this role for a user, go to [User Permissions](../settings/users.md#user-permissions).<br>
