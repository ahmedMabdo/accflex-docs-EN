# General Ledger

### Advantages of General Ledger Program

* **Create and edit Chart of Accounts, and  you can register an endless number of Branches, and Cost Centers at all levels.**
* **Divide the Financial Year, from 1 to 12 Sub Period Divisions.**&#x20;
* **Automatically, run Journal Actions for all the system entries, even entries from other AccFlex programs**, for example; If the salaries were approved by the program of Payroll& HR, automatically, it will registered in the GL program.
* **The General Ledger program, is a Real Time System, making it easy for you to follow up, the traces of each transaction**. for example; Traces of a bill issued from any branch, on the (Income statement, Balance Sheet), is easily observed by the administration, as soon as the bill is issued.
* **General Ledger program offers an integrated financial system, to keep up with the states of the liquidity, profit, and sales rates. Comparison with previous years data in diagrams and illustrations is also possible.**
