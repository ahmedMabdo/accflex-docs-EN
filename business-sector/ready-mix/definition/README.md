# Definition



The **Definitions Modules** form the foundational framework for managing ready-mix concrete operations by standardizing key data across inventory, purchasing, sales, general ledger (GL), and cash control. The **Inventory Definitions** establish material classifications, unit measures, delivery terms and storage locations, ensuring accurate stock tracking. The **Purchase Definitions** set vendors , vendors group, and purchases organizations, streamlining procurement processes. The **Sales Definitions**  customer , and customer groups to maintain consistency in order management. The **GL Definitions** standardize chart of accounts  and cost allocations for accurate financial reporting, while the **Cash Control Definitions** govern payment methods, bank reconciliations, and cash flow tracking. Together, these modules ensure system-wide consistency, operational efficiency, and financial control across the ready-mix business.&#x20;
