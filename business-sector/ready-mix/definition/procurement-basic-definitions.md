# Procurement Basic Definitions

Procurement is the starting point of material flow. In the ready-mix model, raw materials like cement, aggregates, sand, water, and chemicals must be purchased efficiently, tracked properly, and cost accurately. AccFlex ERP manages this through a structured and integrated procurement process, beginning with essential definitions.

·         Purchase Organization: Defines the structure and control the inventory management.

·         Supplier Group: Categorizes suppliers into logical groups for better management, and reporting.

·         Supplier: Defines each individual vendor or supplier from whom goods or services are purchased.
