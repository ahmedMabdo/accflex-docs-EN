# Sales Basic Definitions

**Sales Basic Definitions** in **AccFlex ERP**, which are crucial for managing the sales and delivery process—especially for a ready-mix business where orders are handled per project, client, and delivery location.

·         Sales Organization: Defines the entity or unit within the company that manages sales operations.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Sales Organization.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Sales Organization</p></figcaption></figure>

·         Customer Group: Categorizes customers into logical groups for better management, and reporting.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Customer Group.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Customer Group</p></figcaption></figure>

·         Customers: Stores the full details of each client or customer, used in quotations, orders, deliveries, and billing.
