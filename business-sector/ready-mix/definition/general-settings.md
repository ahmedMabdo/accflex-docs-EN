# General Settings

General setting is where users can define screens like countries, payment terms, cars, drivers, commission policies, and so forth.

· In the **driver screen**, a **driver** is defined as an individual responsible for delivering goods—such as ready-mix concrete—whose information is recorded and managed within the system. The screen captures key details such as the driver's name, license number, end date.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Driver Screen.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Driver Screen</p></figcaption></figure>

Hint: If the driver is marked as an **employee**, they will receive a **bonus** for each completed delivery, which is processed through payroll. If the driver is a **non-employee** or **contractor**, they will receive a **commission** per delivery, which is handled through accounts payable or service payments.

·         The **cars screen**  is used to record and manage important information about each vehicle in the company's fleet.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/car Screen.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Cars Screen</p></figcaption></figure>

·         Driver Commission Setting refers to the configuration within a system that determines how commission payments are calculated and applied for non-employee (contracted) drivers.
